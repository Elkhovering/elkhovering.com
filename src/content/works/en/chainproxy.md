## Context

ChainProxy provides residential and datacenter proxies with a focus on crypto and Web3 users. They came in with a working backend and zero frontend — just an API and a Telegram bot for sales.

## Problem

The existing flow lost users immediately: no landing, no self-serve dashboard, no clear pricing. They needed both a conversion-optimized marketing site and a functional user platform — from scratch, fast.

## My role

Product design lead and frontend. I defined the information architecture, designed the full component system from landing through onboarding and dashboard, and shipped the frontend in React/TypeScript coordinating with their backend dev.

## Process

Started with the user journey — from "I found the link" to "I have working proxies." Designed the purchase flow first (highest-leverage), then the dashboard (retention), then the landing (acquisition). Built a shared design system so both surfaces felt coherent.

## Outcome

Monthly signups increased significantly after launch. The self-serve dashboard reduced support requests by removing the Telegram-bot dependency for common tasks. The platform has since scaled to serve thousands of active users.
