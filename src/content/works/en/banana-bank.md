## Context

Banana Token was launching a meme-fintech coin in 2020. The team needed a landing page that could carry the brand's energy and work as a standalone marketing asset — something the community would share.

## Problem

How do you make a crypto landing that doesn't look like every other crypto landing? The brief was open: lean into the meme energy, go dense with motion, make it impossible to scroll past.

## My role

I handled the full visual concept, designed the layout system, built the animation pipeline, and shipped the frontend solo. The client provided direction on brand tone; execution was entirely mine.

## Process

Started with motion references from outside the crypto space — fashion campaigns, editorial design. Built a custom GSAP pipeline covering scroll-linked parallax, entrance sequences, and hover states. Added a WebGL layer for the hero. Iterated on performance until mobile felt smooth enough.

## Outcome

The landing received strong community pickup at launch and was shared widely in meme-fintech circles. The marketing team cited the site as a key asset in the initial distribution push. The animation density became a reference benchmark for the team's next projects.
