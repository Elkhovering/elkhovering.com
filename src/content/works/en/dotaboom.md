## Context

BetBoom, a major esports betting platform, partnered with the Dota 2 community around The International 2022. The project: an online battlepass where users could complete quests, earn rewards, and climb a leaderboard — all tied to TI match results.

## Problem

TI happens once a year and lasts two weeks. Everything had to be designed, built, and shipped before the event started — and had to handle peak load during the tournament's most-watched matches.

## My role

Art direction, battlepass UX, frontend implementation, and coordination with BetBoom's marketing and backend teams. I was the single creative point-of-contact for the project.

## Process

Designed the quest and reward system first (the core loop), then the leaderboard, then the landing. Built in React with Canvas for the animated elements. The backend team handled TI match data feeds; I built the UI state machine on top. Final performance pass before TI Day 1.

## Outcome

The battlepass ran without incidents through the entire TI 2022 tournament window. Thousands of users completed quests during the event. BetBoom reported strong engagement with the platform feature for the duration of the tournament.
